# FutureMe

## Register

product

## Platform

android

## Users

Self-improvement individuals who want to see where their daily habits are leading them. Habit tracking newcomers looking for motivation through future visualization. Productivity enthusiasts who already track habits and want deeper AI-driven insights and forecasts. The common thread: someone who wants to connect what they do today to who they'll become.

## Product Purpose

FutureMe tracks daily habits and uses AI to predict your future life trajectory. It exists to make the abstract long-term consequences of daily choices visible, tangible, and motivating. Success looks like users internalizing that their habits are building their future, one day at a time.

## Positioning

AI time travel for your habits. See your future self based on what you do today — the only habit tracker that predicts your life trajectory. Your habits, your future, predicted: AI-powered habit tracking that shows you where your current path leads. Make choices your future self will thank you for.

## Brand Personality

Clean, precise, motivating. The app should feel like a well-designed dashboard — sharp, clear, and trustworthy. Not playful or gamified, but quietly confident. The tone is that of a thoughtful guide showing you what's ahead with clarity and care.

## Anti-references

Dark, gamified apps with badges, streaks as the main focus, colorful confetti, and overly playful interactions. FutureMe should not feel like a game or a gimmick — the predictions are serious and should feel that way.

## Design Principles

- **Make the future tangible.** Every screen should connect a present action to a future outcome. The prediction engine is the differentiator; make it feel central, not bolted on.
- **Clarity over decoration.** Clean hierarchy, precise spacing, readable typography. Nothing exists just to fill space. Every element earns its place by carrying meaning.
- **Calm confidence.** The UI stays out of the way. Motion is purposeful and subtle. The app trusts that the data and predictions are compelling enough without flashy chrome.
- **Respect the user's time.** Onboarding is fast, habit entry is frictionless, and the dashboard gives an immediate snapshot. The user should never hunt for what matters.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard best practices: legible text sizes, sufficient contrast, touch-friendly targets. No specific WCAG level mandated beyond what shipping-quality conventions require.
